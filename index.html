About GMA
We create generative media art that transforms data into sensory experiences, using technology to expand aesthetic expression. Our practice critically examines contemporary techno-culture and questions the cultural impacts of technology. We also develop a theoretical framework connecting art, philosophy, and engineering through integrated research.

Connect with us
