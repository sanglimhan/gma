Floating Dream Garden
AI Art, Single Chnnel Video (2000 px * 550 px), 3:00, 2025
"Floating Dream Garden" is a video piece created using artificial intelligence, presenting a landscape where emotion and technology intersect. Instead of reproducing real nature, the work focuses on evoking the sensations that nature can inspire.
Images generated by algorithms flow and shift within a visual rhythm, blending and transforming into a lyrical and dreamlike scene. Digital flowers drift through pixels, suggesting a new kind of nature that moves across time and space. In this unfamiliar yet intimate landscape shaped by technology, viewers encounter fragments of their own memories and emotions.
Here, technology functions not just as a tool but as an artistic language that mediates emotion. "Floating Dream Garden" explores how AI-driven creation can stimulate layers of feeling and memory, and considers the potential of AI art to serve as a new form of poetic expression.
